PANEER MASALA

  1. INGREDIENTS
    • Paneer
    • SPICES : RED CHILLI POWDER,TURMERIC POWDER,GARAM MASALA,SALT,ONION AND GARLIC MASALA,CORIANDER POWDER,KITCHEN KING
    • CORIANDER LEAVES
    • CURRY LEAVES
    • JEERA,MUSTARD
    • 3-4 ONIONS
    • 1-2 TOMATOS
    • CREAM
    • GINGER GARLIC PASTE,OIL,BUTTER
  2. INSTRUCTION
    • Cut paneer into small pieces and fry them little.
    • Firstly, in a large kadai heat 3 tbsp oil and splutter 1 tsp mustard, 1 tsp jeera and few curry leaves.
    • Also add Â¼ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder, 1 tsp coriander powder and 1 tsp garam masala.
    • Saute on low flame till spices turn aromatic.
    • Now add in prepared tomato and onion paste and saute well.
    • Cook till the oil is released from masala paste.
    • Also add 2 cup water and adjust consistency.
    • Cover and boil for 3-4 minutes or till mixture is cooked completely.Then add salt and Paneer pieces.
    • Once the is cooked, oil starts to float indicating misal is ready.
    • In a serving plate, takepaneer masala add some butter and cream over it.
    • Top with chopped coriander leaves over it.
    • Finally, serve PANEER MASALA with roti , rice and lemon wedges making a complete Paneer Masala recipe.
