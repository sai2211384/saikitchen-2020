PAV-BHAJI

  1. INGREDIENTS
    • POTATO,SHIMLA MIRCH,PEAS,COLIFLOWER,BEAT ROOT
    • SPICES : RED CHILLI POWDER,TURMERIC POWDER,GARAM MASALA,PAV-BHAJI MASALA,SALT,
    • CORIANDER LEAVES
    • CURRY LEAVES
    • JEERA,MUSTARD
    • 3-4 ONIONS
    • 1-2 TOMATOS
    • BUTTER,OIL
    • PAV
    • GINGER GARLIC PASTE
  2. INSTRUCTION
    • Take a PRESSURE COOKER and put potato,shimla mirch,peas,coliflower and beat root in it.Then put some water and take 3-4 whistle.
    • Chopped the onion and tomato,take a kadai put some butter and some oil in it and fry onion,tomato,ginger garlic paste.
    • Later put curry leaves,jeera mustard and all the spices in it.Mix it with thw help of spoon.
    • Take out the cooked vegetables in a big plate and smash it with the help of smasher
    • After smashing put all the vegetables in the kadai and again mix it properly.Later add water in the mixture.
    • And cook it for 5-6 minutes.
    • Now your PAV-BHAJI is ready
  3. HOW TO SERVE
    • In a serving plate, take some PAV-BHAJI.
    • Top with chopped onions and coriander leaves over it.
    • Finally, serve PAV-BHAJI with pav and lemon wedges making a complete PAV-BHAJI recipe.
