PINEAPPLE SHEERA(THE FAMOUS FOOD IN MAHARASHTRA)

  1. INGREDIENTS
    • RAVA,PINEAPPLE
    • DRY FRUITS
    • OIL
    • SUGAR
    • PINEAPPLE SYRUP
    • YELLOW FOOD COLOUR
    • WATER
  2. INSTRUCTION
    • Take a kadai and put water in it, let the water boil.
    • Then put oil, dryfruits ,Pineapple syrup and yellow food colour in it.
    • After put chopped pineapple in it and cooked it for 3-4 min.
    • Later put Rava in it and mix it with the help of spoon.
    • Then out dryfruits in it and again mix it.
  3. HOW TO SERVE
    • In a serving plate, take matki usal add some farsan over it.
    • Top with chopped onions and coriander leaves over it.
    • Also, pour ladleful of kat or gravy from sides.
    • Finally, serve misal with pav and lemon wedges making a complete misal pav recipe.
